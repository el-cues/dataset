The Project

The EL-CUES database was created to provide a commercially usable alternative to existing image databases for emotional stimulation. It provides annotations for emotional scenes in the circumplex space (Valence, Arousal) and for basic emotions after Paul Ekman.

Selector

The El-CUES Selector is a Tool that lets you customize your selection of images to the precise emotion you want to evoke, and to age and gender of the participant.
